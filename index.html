Who We Are

Financial expertise,
human at heart

We're a Colombo-based accountancy firm that combines deep technical expertise with a genuine commitment to our clients' success.

Founded in 2010, Commercial Accountancy Service has grown from a boutique tax practice into a full-spectrum financial firm. We work with businesses across Sri Lanka — from early-stage startups to multi-branch enterprises — delivering the kind of proactive, personalised service that larger firms rarely offer.

Our team of chartered accountants and financial advisors brings together decades of cross-industry experience. We don't just file returns and close books — we ask the questions that unlock opportunity.
